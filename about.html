About

This website is a digital collection of the thoughts, ideas, projects and questions that a certain human decided to make public for the world one day, to prompt himself to do some more valuable cognitive work.

That human happens to be me, Aman Patel. I identify most closely as an inquisitive and occasionally-emotional citizen, but I'm also the leader of Grove, a coordinator for OneShared.World, a student at the University of Southern California, and an aspiring effective altruist, among other things.

I'm intersted pretty much everything under the sun, and most things over and inside the sun too: neuroscience and physics and computer science (particularly theoretical), mathematics (particularly meta), philosophy (particularly of epistemology, ethics and consciousness), complex systems, political economy, mental health, medicine... you get the idea. In short, imagine Sid the Science Kid was a little bit older and didn't have purple hair--and you get a pretty good description of me.

I think humans can create an immensely beautiful future if we figure out how to organize ourselves well, and I'm excited to be a part of this endeavor.

If you want to understand what makes me tick, read this. If you're interested instead in the document I use to perpetuate socially-enforced meritocratic judgment, here's my resume.
