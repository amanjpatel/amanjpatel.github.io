About

This website is a digital collection of the thoughts, ideas, projects and questions that a certain human decided to make public for the world one day, to prompt itself to do some more valuable cognitive work.

That human happens to be me, Aman Patel. I identify most closely as an inquisitive and occasionally-emotional citizen of Earth, but I'm also the leader of Grove, a student at the University of Southern California, and an a wannabe effective altruist, among other things.

I'm intersted pretty much everything under the sun, and most things over and inside the sun too: neuroscience and physics and computer science (particularly theoretical), mathematics (particularly meta), philosophy (particularly of epistemology, ethics and consciousness), complex systems, political economy, mental health, medicine... you get the idea. In short, imagine Sid the Science Kid was a little bit older, more empathetic, and didn't have purple hair--and you get a pretty good description of me.
